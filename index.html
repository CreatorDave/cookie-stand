Welcome to the official Salmon Cookies website! Take a swim around our site...

The process of making our world famous cookies starts with the catch. We only pick our salmon from the clean lakes across the nation, and pick out the best ones. Using state of the art technology, we compress the ingredients together in the shape of salmon with our shaper tools, let them bake, and finally frost and decorate each individual cookie to perfection!

Hello there! My name is Pat, founder of Salmon Cookies™. Me and my team strive to make the best cookies around, with fresh salmon caught from our local lake everyday. Using only the best ingredients to make both an amazing looking, and tasting cookie that wont have a "fishy" after taste! We test every batch of cookies on live salmon before sending them to our stores across the globe so you can be assured of our quality. So go ahead, take a bite of our incredible salmon cookies, we know you will love every bite! - Pat Salmondo
